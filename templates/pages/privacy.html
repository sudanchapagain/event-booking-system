Privacy policy

Introduction

At Sano Chautari Software Company Limited, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our software, Chautari. By using our platform, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services. This includes:
Personal Information: When you register or sign in to Chautari, we may collect personal details such as your name, email address, and contact information.
Usage Data: We collect data about how you interact with the platform, including your search queries, event preferences, and browsing history.
Device Information: Information about the device you use to access Chautari, such as IP address, browser type, and operating system, may be collected.



How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:
Provide and personalize our services to enhance your experience with Chautari.
Analyze usage patterns to improve the platform's functionality and performance.
Communicate with you about updates, new features, and other relevant information.
Ensure the security of our platform and protect against unauthorized access or activities.



Data Sharing and Disclosure

We respect your privacy and do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share your data with third parties only in the following situations:
With your consent or at your direction.
To comply with legal obligations, such as responding to a subpoena or court order.
To protect the rights, property, or safety of Sano Chautari Software Company Limited, our users, or others.

We implement robust security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy.
